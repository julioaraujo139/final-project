    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is pest control?

    Pest control is the process of applying specific chemical products to eliminate or control insects, rodents, and other harmful organisms in a given environment.

    When should I consider pest control?

    Pest control is recommended when there is a pest infestation that poses a risk to health or the environment. This may include infestations of insects such as cockroaches, ants, fleas, termites, among others.

    What are the risks associated with the presence of pests?

    Pests can cause material damage, transmit diseases and allergies, and affect people’s quality of life. In addition, insects like termites can cause structural damage to buildings.

    Is it safe for children and pets?

    Most pest control companies use safe products when applied correctly. However, it is important to follow the instructions provided by the professional and take precautions, such as keeping children and pets away from the treated area during the recommended period.

    How long does pest control last?

    The effectiveness duration varies depending on the pest type, environment, and products used. In general, pest control provides protection from a few weeks to several months.
